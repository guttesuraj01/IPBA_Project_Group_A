About the Data
The data set is Sales data with Customer and Geography breakup of Adidas Sales, records available 9648 useful for unsupervised clustering of data on the customer's records from a Adidas Shoes Sale database. Customer segmentation is the practice of separating customers into groups that reflect similarities among customers in each cluster. I will divide customers into segments to optimize the significance of each customer to the business. To modify products according to distinct needs and behaviours of the customers. It also helps the business to cater to the concerns of different types of customers.
